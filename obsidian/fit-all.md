# Fit all

Fit-all moves and zooms the [[viewport]] so that every window on the
[[infinite-canvas]] is visible at once. It is bound to `Super+0`.

Fit-all computes the bounding box of all windows in [[world-coordinates]] and
chooses the camera position and [[zoom]] that frame that box.

It is the inverse of getting lost on the canvas: it is the quick way to see
everything and re-orient, complementing [[directional-focus]] and the reset bind.
